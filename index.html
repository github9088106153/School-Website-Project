DON BOSCO SCHOOL BANDEL

PRINCIPAL'S MESSAGE

Dear Staff, Students, Parents and Well-wishers, Welcome to this website of Don Bosco School Bandel. As you go through the site you can see that it has been painstakingly prepared to present the various facets of this school to which many of you are associated either as students, parents or admirers. It is not inappropriate to mention that you are close to Don Bosco School because of the values that the school pledges in its life and activities. The school has always held aloft the teachings of Jesus Christ who says “I have come that they may have life and have it in abundance”. Thus, we are doubly blessed to be associated with this institution that claims to be a beacon to the people who live on both sides of the Hooghly River and radiating the teaching of Christ which assures us of life and life in abundance.
Don Bosco School pledges to make all who enter into its portal into persons of character, competence, conscience, compassion and commitment, who will foster uprightness in public life.
I invite all persons of good will and all who are associated with this institution to beacons in their own settings and thus carry the legacy they have received from being associated with this institution. God bless you and may Don Bosco guide us.
Yours truly
Fr. Siby Joseph Vadakel
Principal
